Turnaround Times

What is the standard lead time for bulk orders? expand_more

Our standard lead time for most textile products ranges between 4 to 6 weeks from the date of final design approval. For specialized technical fabrics or high-volume apparel contracts, this may extend to 8-10 weeks to ensure rigorous quality assurance at every stage of the weave.

Do you offer expedited production? expand_more

Yes, we offer priority manufacturing slots for time-sensitive projects. Expedited turnaround can reduce lead times by up to 40%, depending on current capacity. Please contact our logistics team to discuss surcharge rates and available windows.

International Shipping

Which countries do you currently serve? expand_more

While we operate primary hubs in Canada and Australia, we provide global logistics solutions serving the USA, European Union, and select markets in Southeast Asia. Our shipping partners specialize in delicate textile handling and international customs clearance.

How are freight costs calculated? expand_more

Freight costs are calculated based on a combination of volumetric weight, destination zone, and desired transit speed. We offer both EXW (Ex Works) and DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) options for our corporate clients to simplify the importation process.

Quality Certifications

Are your materials OEKO-TEX certified? expand_more

Absolutely. Every roll of fabric that leaves our facility is OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certified, ensuring that no harmful substances are present in our finished goods. We also hold GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard) certification for our organic linen and cotton blends.
